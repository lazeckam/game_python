Menu:
Using up-down arrows change bars, using left-right arrows change 
level options and number of lives. 
While choosing a start/quit option, click the Space to confirm.

How to play?
 - you can control the player using left, right and up arrows
 - the aim of the game is to collect as many coins as it is possible.
 - all bugs and insects etc. are your enemies - when you touch them, 
   you lose one life.
 - you can kill your enemies jumping on them
 - collect keys to open cubbies by hitting them with your head. 
   They will not open without a key!
 - mushroom effects: the red one makes you walk faster, while
   the brown one spoil this ability
